Horizontal Tabs V1.0.0 Quick Start Guide  
Horizontal tabs is an addon that allows to use sidebar panels as popover menus in 
Blender’s native header space available for various editors.
 
 
 
 
 
 

--- Page 2 ---
How to install 
 
1. Navigate to blender preferences -> Add-ons tab and select the dropdown. 
 
 
 
 
2. From the dropdown select the option “Install from Disk…” 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
3. Navigate to where you have downloaded the .zip file for the add-on 

--- Page 3 ---
 
 
4. Select it and then click “Install from Disk”  
 
 
Once installed 
 
All your add-ons from the sidebar will appear in Blender’s header 
 
If you have a lot of add-ons you can scroll on the header with the mouse 
wheel to go through them. 
 
Moreover, you can tweak preferences for Horizontal Tabs in the preferences 
menu. To do this: 
 
1. Navigate to Blender preferences add-ons tab. 
2. Look for Horizontal Tabs.  

--- Page 4 ---
 
3. Expand the add-on and find all settings related to it. 
4. Scroll down to see all your sidebar panels and per editor settings. 
Finally, let’s review the different operators available in the preferences.  

--- Page 5 ---
Operators 
The eye (Visibility icon) next to entries 
 
 
Allows to toggle visibility of add-ons on or off. 
The Arrow operators next to the entries list 
 
Move entries left or right in the header.  
Up = Left,  
Down = Right. 

--- Page 6 ---
Tab settings context menu 
 
 
Click the small arrow to open the Tab settings context menu 
 
These settings will be changed per panel. Uncheck “Use Default Panel Width to 
override it manually as needed. 
 
 
 
 

--- Page 7 ---
The Editor Visibility Toggle 
 
 
The global visibility toggle icon is the eye icon located on the left side of the Horizontal 
Tabs entries. 
  
On by default: Shows the header entries for the add-on. 
Off: Hides panel entries from the current editor.
